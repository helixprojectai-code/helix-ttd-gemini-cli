**Status:** ✅ Archived & Canonical | **Objective:** Record and publish the monthly and yearly carbon emissions associated with the OVHcloud baremetal substrate hosting the Helix-Core habitat — providing transparent, location-based and market-based metrics for manufacturing, electricity, and operations to support environmental accountability and sustainability tracking.

## 🔍 Investigation / Summary
This report details the carbon footprint of the OVHcloud baremetal server (ADV-III, Beauharnois datacenter) for December 2025 and the full year 2025. Total emissions: 32 kg CO₂eq (location-based) and 27 kg CO₂eq (market-based) for the month; 123 kg CO₂eq and 105 kg CO₂eq for the year. The data is rounded to the nearest integer and includes methodological notes in appendix. The report underscores OVHcloud's sustainability initiatives and provides clear visibility into the environmental impact of the sovereign substrate.
