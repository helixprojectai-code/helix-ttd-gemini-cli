## 🔍 Investigation / Summary
The Helix Governance Calendar enforces a predictable, annual cadence for critical activities: Q1 hardening & audits, Q2 rotation & resilience, Q3 expansion & standards, and Q4 constitutional convention. It includes mandatory red team reviews, infrastructure drills, role rotations, transparency reports, and amendment windows to prevent entropy and ensure the Council remains vigilant.
