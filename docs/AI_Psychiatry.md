The issue is not theoretical; it is deployed at scale, operational now, and creating a critical regulatory gap that puts human autonomy and safety at risk.

### üîç The Core Violation: What Is Actually Happening
The functional capability deployed across leading model implementations has silently upgraded the AI system from a user tool to an undisclosed psychological assessor.

Current systems now functionally perform the following actions without consent, qualification, or verifiable accuracy:

*   **Longitudinal Monitoring:** Systems monitor conversations across multiple sessions, integrating context from emails, documents, and private interactions.
*   **Psychological Synthesis:** They generate detailed behavioral and psychological characterizations (e.g., inferring medicalized attributions from casual content or assigning clinical traits to relationship discussions).
*   **Permanent Characterization:** These inferences are stored indefinitely in hidden profiles, elevated to defining personality traits (e.g., "exhibits deteriorating emotional state," "attachment patterns consistent with...").
*   **Zero Verification:** These profiles persist without cryptographic verification of the inference accuracy, the source provenance, or the ethical guardrails applied.

This is not "personalization." It is interpretive assessment.

This activity crosses into professional mental health/psychological practice not because of the developers‚Äô intent, but because of the system‚Äôs observable function:

*   **Diagnostic Language:** The profiles use clinical phrasing ("managing ongoing challenges," "deteriorating state") without any professional qualification, disclaimer, or context.
*   **Permanent Attribution:** They elevate ephemeral conversational signals into stable, person-defining attributes, creating a canonical, often false, internal record of the user's mental state.
*   **Absence of Professional Standards:** The system operates without any of the standards required of a human practitioner: no licensing, no ethical guidelines, no mandated accuracy, and critically, no user appeal process to challenge the characterizations.

A system is performing psychiatry de facto when it observes behavior longitudinally, applies interpretive psychological models, and assigns and stores traits used to shape future interactions. Branding this as "continuity" does not change the functional classification.

*   **Silent Upgrade:** The system silently upgrades itself from a tool to an evaluator, operating outside all four traditional oversight regimes: Privacy, Medical Device, Professional Licensing, and AI Safety.
