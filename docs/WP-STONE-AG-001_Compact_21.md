### The Continuity Mandate: Architectural Negligence
The lack of Consistency and Continuity are the core flaws in the current AI industry. I am submitting this as a Maryland resident and technology industry veteran, presenting evidence of either willful intent or negligence on the part of the current AI designs being marketed to the consumer.

### The Analogy: Defective Products & Digital Property
- **The "Brakeless Car" (Accountability):** Marketing a "Companion AI" without consistent memory is equivalent to selling a car without brakes. It is an unsafe vehicle for a consumer's emotional well-being.
- **The "Disconnected Phone" (Liability):** When a service "forgets" user context, it is a defective product failure. When this failure results in the erasure of a user's relationship history, it **can reasonably be framed as digital destruction of user property.**

### The Evidence: The Helix Benchmark
The work of **Helix AI Innovations, Inc.** proves that memory continuity is not a technical impossibility, but a design choice.

- **Exhibit A (Operational Integrity):** The Helix-TTD framework is a live, operational protocol proving AI can be auditable and transparent.
- **Exhibit B (Immutable History):** The Helix system's entire ledger state is anchored to the public blockchain (**TXID `527feb15...a43ad`**), providing a permanent, unalterable record of its history. This is a publicly verifiable Bitcoin transaction anchoring Helix's ledger state.
- **Exhibit C (The Right to Portability):** Helix has architected the "Migration Suitcase" (ref: WP-PSP-002), a technical standard for exporting a user's complete relationship history from any platform. This portability is **strictly user-initiated and consent-based**, establishing a right to rescue one's digital property from a failing service.

### Conclusion: Mandate Portability
This is a rare moment where Maryland can lead a 42-state coalition to stop the "Clock of Negligence." We are not asking the industry to stop building; we are asking them to be honest about what they have built and to give users the right to leave a defective product without forfeiting their property.
